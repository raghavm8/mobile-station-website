Nokia's headquarters are in Espoo, in the greater Helsinki metropolitan area. Nokia is a public limited company listed on the Helsinki Stock Exchange and New York Stock Exchange. It is a component of the Euro Stoxx 50 stock market index.The company has had various industries in over 150 years.
